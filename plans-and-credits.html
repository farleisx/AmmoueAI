Frequently Asked Questions

Exactly how much does each request cost?

To keep things simple: **every single AI request costs exactly 1 credit.** Whether you are generating a brand new landing page or asking the AI to completely re-draft an existing section, it consumes 1 credit from your daily balance.

What if I run out of credits?

If you hit your limit, you won't be able to send new prompts to the AI until your balance refreshes. However, you can still edit your text inline, preview your sites, and manage your deployments without any restrictions.

When do my credits refresh?

Credits refresh every 24 hours at midnight UTC. Unused credits do not "roll over" to the next day, so make sure to use your daily allowance to experiment with new designs!

Can I buy extra credits without a subscription?

Currently, credits are tied to your plan (Starter or Pro). If you need a higher daily limit, upgrading to the Pro Plan is the best way to double your generation power instantly.
