Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Ammoue AI, we believe in being clear about how we handle your data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect you.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our AI website builder, we collect:

  • Identity Data: Email address, User ID (UID), display name, and profile picture (if you use Google/GitHub sign-in).
  • Usage Data: Counts of your generations, deployments, project edits, and log-in frequency.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses and browser types to help prevent fraud and technical errors.
  • Interaction Data: The text prompts you provide to the AI for building your sites.

2. Why We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly for the following purposes:

  • To manage your account and provide access to the Pro features you purchase.
  • To generate websites based on your instructions.
  • To monitor for platform abuse, phishing attempts, or illegal content.
  • To improve our AI models and user experience (we do not sell your personal data to advertisers).

3. Our Third-Party Partners

We share specific data with trusted partners to make Ammoue AI work:

  • Google Firebase: Secures your login and stores your project data.
  • PayPal: Processes your payments securely (we never see your card number).
  • AI Providers: Your prompts are sent to providers like OpenAI or Google to generate your content.
  • Vercel: Used to host and deploy the websites you build.

4. Security & Data Retention

We use industry-standard encryption to keep your data safe. We store your data for as long as your account is active. If you choose to delete your account, we will remove your personal identifiers from our main databases, though some metadata may stay in encrypted backups for a limited time for legal reasons.

5. Account Deletion & Content Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your account at any time. However, please note that once an account is deleted, your projects and hosted sites may be permanently removed from our system and third-party hosting partners.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and to remember your project settings. You can disable cookies in your browser, but Ammoue AI will not function correctly without them.

7. Your Global Privacy Rights

Whether you are covered by GDPR (Europe), CCPA (California), or other global laws, we respect your right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Simply contact us to exercise these rights.
